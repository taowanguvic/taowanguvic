TAO WANG

Welcome to my website! In July 2022, I will be an assistant professor in the Department of Economics at the University of Victoria, British Columbia. I like conceiving and developing novel research questions that are generally based on ideas in econometrics, statistics, and machine learning. My current research concentrates on building and developing a broad variety of parametric and nonparametric modal regression models and investigating their statistical inference with possible applications.
